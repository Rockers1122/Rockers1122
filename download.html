Hostel
  • Hostel
GURU SAMUDRA INDER HOSTEL

The college has a spacious in-built hostel comprising of 350 seats. All the rooms are attached with bathrooms and are centrally air cooled, having Almira’s and study tables for their incumbents. The hostel campus is equipped with facilities like Solar Geysers, Aqua guard, Generator, CCTV Cameras to monitor the in and out going and other activities of the boarders. The hostel also provides some fun activities during the evening time. Girls play many games out there as it has a big sports complex.

Hostel mess is spacious with sufficient dinning furniture.

It provides hygienic & nutritious food. The students decide the weekly menu according to which meals are prepared & served. The mess staff is well aware of the importance of the quality of food.

RANJANA
HOSTEL WARDEN

Hostel accommodation is provided with the understanding that the resident student will strictly abide by the Hostel Rules currently in force or as may be enforced from time to time. Accommodation in the Hostel cannot be claimed as a matter of right. The Institute administration may refuse accommodation to any student who is known to have grossly violated the Hostel Rules or whose presence is likely to disturb the peace and tranquility of hostel

Students must remember that hostel is the home of the student on the campus and therefore, she should behave on the campus as well as outside in such a manner as to bring credit to her and to the Institution. She must observe them strictly. Ignorance of rules will not be considered as an excuse.

Every student should stay in the room allotted to her. Boarders must look up the Hostel Notice Board regularly. Hostel students shall not leave the campus without prior permission of the Warden In-charge/Warden. Room furniture and electric fittings are required to be maintained by the inmates in good condition. Students should lock their room properly when they go out for bath, food etc. Ragging in any form is a cognizable offence and severely punishable as per the Supreme Court directives. Guests are not permitted to stay overnight in the room of the students. Hostlers spoiling the college property will be liable to disciplinary actions.

Silence & discipline must be observed in the classroom as well as in the entire campus of the institution. A student must maintain a high standard of discipline and abide by the rules and regulations of the college. Ragging in any form in the college campus is prohibited.
